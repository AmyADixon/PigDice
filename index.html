Game Rules

Starting with player 1, both players will continue to roll for numbers to add to their scores. All numbers may be added to the total score, but 1 . If a 1 is rolled, then the player who rolled it will both forfeit their turn and the current points accumulated during the turn (0 will be added to their total). Pass may be utilized to switch players before a 1 is rolled, but less points may be collected as a result; it's a gamble. The first player to accumulate 100 points wins the game.
